About Octavio Serrago

Octavio Serrago born in Argentina, is a country music singer, songwriter and guitarist. He went through some musical bands but his musical career started when he decided to be a solo artist. Since he was a child he listened to willie nelson, merle haggard, johnny cash, among other great artists of the genre. In 2021 he released his debut single called "The First Day of Spring" and in 2022 he released his first ep "Lost". You can find him on all platforms.
